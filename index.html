Janitaur

Custodian of Chaos. Relic of Space Time. Builder of Futures.

Janitaur emerged from a Peregian lab accident involving an LLM cluster, a fossilised femur, and an unattended mop bucket. The experiment sought to train an AI to clean datasets. It instead produced a custodial saurian with unusually high agency.

He is the Hub's living contradiction: ancient instincts fused with frontier computation. Built to tidy mess, compelled to construct worlds.
